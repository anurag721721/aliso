Periodontal Dentistry

Comprehensive Oral Care for Every Stage of Life

OWe specialize in gentle, personalized dental care for infants, children, and teens. From a child’s first tooth to their teenage years, our goal is to create a comfortable, fun, and educational environment for every visit.

Top Reasons

Why Choose Us?

  • Kid-friendly and calming environment designed just for children
  • Experienced, board-certified Periodontal dentists
  • Comfortable treatment rooms with fun themes
  • Parents welcome during exams and treatments
  • Focus on education to build lifelong healthy habits
  • Flexible scheduling and emergency care available

Infant Oral Health Exams

Gentle exams for babies to monitor development and guide parents on early dental care.

Preventive Care

Protect young teeth with cleanings, fluoride treatments, and dental sealants.

Digital X-rays

Safe, low-radiation imaging to detect dental issues early and accurately.

Tooth-Colored Fillings

Natural-looking fillings to repair cavities while keeping your child’s smile bright.

Emergency Dental Care

Quick, compassionate care for dental injuries, toothaches, and accidents.

Sedation Dentistry

Safe sedation options to help anxious children feel calm and comfortable.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accept my insurance?
Yes — we accept most major dental insurance plans. If you’re unsure, feel free to call us and we’ll be happy to verify your coverage.
Do you speak Spanish?
Absolutely! Our staff is bilingual and happy to assist you in both English and Spanish
Are you accepting new patients?
Absolutely! We provide gentle, friendly periodontal dental care for children of all ages.
What can I expect at my first visit?
Your first visit includes a full exam, X-rays if needed, and a conversation with the dentist about your health and goals.
Do you offer payment plans?
Yes — we offer flexible payment options for treatments not fully covered by insurance.
