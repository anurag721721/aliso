Everything You Need to Know

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accept my insurance?
Yes — we accept most major dental insurance plans. If you’re unsure, feel free to call us and we’ll be happy to verify your coverage.
Do you speak Spanish?
Absolutely! Our staff is bilingual and happy to assist you in both English and Spanish.
Are you accepting new patients?
Yes, we welcome new patients! You can call or schedule online — we’ll walk you through everything.
What can I expect at my first visit?
Your first visit includes a full exam, X-rays if needed, and a conversation with the dentist about your health and goals.
Do you offer payment plans?
Yes — we offer flexible payment options for treatments not fully covered by insurance.
